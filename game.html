Calibration Instructions

  1. To perform calibration, user needs to provide webcam access. Once you click on Perform Calibration, you accept to allow webcam access.
  2. After clicking on Perform Calibration, wait for the video feed to load.
  3. Once the video has loaded, hold up a high contrast spherical object (e.g., tennis ball, tomato) and click directly on the object in the video feed to begin tracking.
  4. A blue dot will track the object you selected.
  5. If tracking is inaccurate, click "Redo" and try again.
  6. Click "Confirm Selection" when you're satisfied.
